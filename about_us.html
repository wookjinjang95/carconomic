What is Carconomic?

Carconomic is a website that provides the vehicle data analysis tool to help consumers purchase vehicles smarter.

As a consumer, we don't have tools or knowledge of how vehicle market works and how depreciation and market trend affects the price of the vehicle in the future. Using Carconomic Dashboard, you can see the trend of the vehicle you are interested purchasing. By looking at our market report, you can predict the depreciation cost of the vehicle at certain miles.

Overall, cost awareness and resale value prediction are the keys Carconomic dashboard is providing. To see more details and how you can use our dashboard to bring insights to your future vehicle purchase, please feel free to visit our tutorial page for more!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use Carconomic to make a better vehicle purchase?

There are many ways to look into our dashboard data analysis and make smarter purchase. One of the tool that Carconomic recommend is using the CHECK PRICE section in our dashboard.

By selecting the trim, the price that the dealership is offering, and the mileage that the current car has, you can generate a report of our decision to see if the car you are looking at is in good price or not. If it's not a good price, you can see what is the expected price.

Can I request other vehicle data analysis?

Of course! You are definitely welcome to contact Wookjin Jang, who is the main developer of Carconomic website directly to request more data for other vehicles. For example, you can simply ask Hey Wookjin, I am currently interested in BMW M3! Can you please upload new data for BMW M3 into the dashboard? Thanks!

To Request, please email: wookjinjang95@gmail.com

How often does the data get updated?

Currently there is no specific date that data gets uploaded to latest. However, you are free to request Carconomic members for latest data and we are welcome to help you to upload the latest data as soon as possible. For now, we generally update once or twice every week to keep it updated.

Where does this data come from?

Currently the data is coming from Carfax website. We collect various information such as VIN, Year, Model, Trim, Mileage and more. Carconomic is willing to research and try to get more data that's not from Carfax to improve our analysis and prediction. If you have a specific website that you want Carconomic to scrape data into, please feel free to contact one of our members for more.
