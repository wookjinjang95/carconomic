Our Mission

To bring data analysis accessible and free for anyone.

We believe that data should be free to everyone and not limited. Currently, big corporations are taking advantage of using Big Data to maxmize profit. However, all these data are coming from individuals who are using the platform. It is not equitable environment where data providers don't have same access to Big Data but corporations do.

Our Story

A.I.F.A stands for Artificial Intelligence for All. Most people don't know much about A.I and how it works. It's because A.I is complicated and not many people studies in A.I field. But most importantly, A.I is not being used in our daily lives. Not only people don't know A.I but we are not aware of it.

Why Vehicle Industry?

As a lead of AIFA, I personally felt that consumers are having hard time purchasing vehicles. To consumers, a vehicle can be secondary home for a family, or a product that brings joy and excitement. However, to to purhcase a single car is difficult. Many platforms like Carfax and CarWoW tells consumers if the price is above average or below average pricing. But we want to know more. This is where data analysis will come and help.

Data Collection
Price vs Miles

Currently AIFA is collceting prices and miles of each vehicles using third party data such as Carfax and other source of websites. The data gets refreshed twice every 2 days. The data is collected carefully and AIFA is trying our best to update the latest data into our dashboard.

Maintenance Data

Maintenance Data of all vehicles are collected through CarMD API. It's a third party company that provides real-time maintenance report for all vehicles. Using this data, AIFA can create many statistical analysis and help consumers to predict their future maintenance cost.
