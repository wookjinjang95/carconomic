Our Mission

To bring data analysis accessible and free for everyone.

We believe that data should be free to everyone and not limited. Currently, big corporations are already using Big Data to analyize and bring insight to maximize its profits. However, all these data are coming from individuals who are using the platform. It is not equitable environment where data providers don't have equal access to Big Data that corporations do.

Our Story

Most people don't know much about Artificial Intelligence (A.I for short) and how it works. It is simply due to the fact that A.I is not practical. But most importantly, A.I is not user-friendly for regular consumers to utilize. Carconomic believes that it is crucial for ordinary people to utilize this A.I tool to benefit themselves in their daily lives.

Why Vehicle Industry?

Around average of 38,356 cars are sold every day. This also means that every day, there are 38,356 of people putting a huge down payment to purcahse vehicles. Unlike other purchases, car purchase is a huge commitment. To help people to make smarter purchases, I, as a lead of carconomic, felt that most consumers are not aware or told of insights such as depreciation and residual values. Many platforms like Carfax and CarWoW tells consumers if the price is above average or below average pricing. However, we want to know WHY. This is where data analysis will come and help. This is where Carconomic will guide you to those WHYs.

How do we collect Data?
Price vs Miles

Currently Carconomic is collceting prices and miles of each vehicles using third party data such as Carfax and other source of websites. The data gets refreshed twice every 2 days. The data is collected carefully and precisely to provide accurate analysis. Carconomic is trying our best to update the latest data into our dashboard.

Maintenance Data

Maintenance Data of all vehicles are collected through CarMD API. It's a third party company that provides real-time maintenance report for all vehicles. Using this data, Carconomic can create many statistical analysis and help consumers to predict their future maintenance cost.
